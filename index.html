About Us

ONE of our goals is to provide an analytical and authenticated electronical health record of a citizen of any region, our target is to keep track of the users health details/medical reports starting from birth. With the aid of the app we ensure that the bio data of the user is kept confidential and accurate as we tend to encourage hospitals to adopt this digital standard of health recording.

We equally try to curb, ameliorate and prevent the rate of occurrence of certain health issues like cancer. To achieve this, we actually looking at away of integrating cancer detection AI into our app which aids in predicting and notifying from previous health records, the user if he or she might likely develop cancer. The cancer detector AI will work perfectly if the user constantly makes use of the app for health check up and records.

The MEDBIO project supports other functionality of which one is adopt me service, due to the fact that only registered, verified,authorized and practicing Doctors will have access to a user account,if given permission by the user. thus, only qaulified Doctors are given this permission inorder to help childless couple who wish to adopt a newly born baby within their vicinity, thereby reducing the number of kids at orphanage homes.

Moreover, other features like, locating/tracking the nearest hospital within your locality would be implemented as well.
users can also view the highly rated hospital in terms of quality of their services.
